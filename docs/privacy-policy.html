Privacy Policy for Pockets Chrome Extension

This Privacy Policy describes how the Pockets Chrome Extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our link-saving extension.

By using the Pockets extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Permissions We Use

The Pockets extension requires certain permissions to function properly. Below is a detailed explanation of each permission and why it is needed:

Storage

We use the storage permission to save your links locally in your browser. This allows you to access your saved links across browser sessions. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

ActiveTab

The activeTab permission allows the extension to access information about the currently active tab when you interact with the extension. This is used to retrieve the URL and title of the page you want to save. This permission only activates when you explicitly use the extension and does not allow background access to your browsing activity.

Context Menus

We use the contextMenus permission to add a "Save to Pockets" option to your right-click menu. This provides a convenient way to save links directly from any webpage without opening the extension popup.

Notifications

The notifications permission is used to display browser notifications when a link is successfully saved or when there are important updates. You can disable these notifications in your browser settings at any time.

Scripting

The scripting permission allows the extension to execute scripts on web pages when you choose to save a link. This is used to extract relevant information such as the page title and URL for your saved links. Scripts are only executed when you explicitly trigger the save action.

Information We Collect

The Pockets extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • URLs of links you choose to save
  • Page titles of saved links
  • Date and time when links are saved

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All your saved links remain on your local device.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API. Your saved links are only accessible to you and are not shared with any third parties. We do not have access to your saved links or browsing history.

Third-Party Services

The Pockets extension does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services. Your data remains entirely on your device.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can view all your saved links at any time through the extension
  • You can delete individual links or all saved links
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

The Pockets extension is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Pockets extension, please contact us at pockets@msar.me.
